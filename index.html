Font Manipulator app.
This app is used to change the size of the text provided in the input box.
HOWTO - To change the size of the text, just clutch your hands and imagine you are crushing or pulling an item, and move it back and forth to change the size.
This may not be reliable, as this is under development. Please excuse any bugs, I will be sure to fix it later.
